# Day 1 Delivery (D1D) Implementation Guide

## Overview

Day 1 Delivery (D1D) creates a bar-raising AWS customer journey through what we deliver and how we deliver it. This guide provides practical implementation steps for maintaining a Day 1 mindset throughout the delivery lifecycle.

## Day 1 Mindset Principles

### Core Characteristics
- **Curious**: Always learning, questioning assumptions, seeking deeper understanding
- **Inventive**: Creative problem-solving, innovative approaches, thinking outside the box
- **Scrappy**: Resourceful execution, efficient use of resources, pragmatic solutions
- **Fast**: Rapid delivery without compromising quality, quick decision-making

### Cultural Foundation
We stay committed to transitioning knowledge to better serve our customers. This requires:
- Continuous learning and adaptation
- Proactive problem identification and resolution
- Customer-centric decision making
- Collaborative team dynamics

## #OneTeam Customer Experience Flywheel

### Central Focus: Customer Experience
All activities revolve around delivering exceptional customer experiences through:
- Seamless collaboration across all stakeholders
- Aligned expectations and shared objectives
- Proactive communication and transparency
- Consistent quality delivery

### Flywheel Components
1. **Customer Engagement**: Deep understanding of customer needs and objectives
2. **Team Alignment**: Unified approach across sales, delivery, and support teams
3. **Quality Delivery**: Consistent, bar-raising implementation execution
4. **Continuous Improvement**: Learning from each engagement to enhance future delivery
5. **Relationship Building**: Strengthening partnerships through successful outcomes

## Implementation Framework

### Phase 1: Sales to Delivery (S2D) Transition

#### Objectives
- **Seamless Customer Experience**: Smooth transition from sales to delivery team
- **Accelerated Delivery**: Rapid engagement startup with comprehensive context
- **Consistent Governance**: Established processes and standards
- **Knowledge Transfer**: Effective information sharing between teams
- **Risk Mitigation**: Proactive identification and planning

#### Key Activities
- [ ] **Customer History Review**: Comprehensive understanding of customer journey
- [ ] **Stakeholder Mapping**: Identify key decision makers and influencers
- [ ] **Goals Alignment**: Validate customer business outcomes and expectations
- [ ] **Risk Assessment**: Document known issues and mitigation strategies
- [ ] **Team Introductions**: Facilitate relationship building across teams

#### Success Criteria
- Delivery team has complete understanding of customer context
- All stakeholders are aligned on objectives and approach
- Communication protocols are established
- Risk mitigation strategies are in place
- Customer confidence in delivery team is established

### Phase 2: Launch and Sprint 0

#### Sprint 0 Objectives
Complete foundational work in the two weeks prior to customer kickoff:
- [ ] **Team Onboarding**: Ensure all team members are prepared and aligned
- [ ] **Governance Setup**: Establish project management and communication structures
- [ ] **Tooling Configuration**: Set up necessary tools and access permissions
- [ ] **Process Definition**: Define team norms and working agreements
- [ ] **Risk Planning**: Develop comprehensive risk management approach

#### Customer Kickoff Requirements
- [ ] **Project Charter**: Clear scope, objectives, and success criteria
- [ ] **Team Introductions**: Roles, responsibilities, and contact information
- [ ] **Communication Plan**: Meeting cadences, reporting structures, escalation paths
- [ ] **Timeline Review**: Milestones, dependencies, and critical path analysis
- [ ] **Success Metrics**: Measurable outcomes and acceptance criteria

### Phase 3: Manage & Inspect

#### Agile Ways of Working
- **Daily Stand-ups**: Progress updates, impediment identification, team coordination
- **Sprint Planning**: Backlog refinement, capacity planning, commitment setting
- **Retrospectives**: Continuous improvement, lesson capture, process optimization
- **Stakeholder Syncs**: Regular alignment, feedback collection, expectation management

#### Quality Assurance
- [ ] **Delivery Quality Inspection (DQI)**: Regular self-assessment of delivery governance
- [ ] **Milestone Reviews**: Progress validation against customer business outcomes
- [ ] **Risk Monitoring**: Proactive identification and mitigation of emerging risks
- [ ] **Customer Satisfaction**: Ongoing feedback collection and response

#### Escalation Management
- **Structured Process**: Clear escalation paths and decision-making authority
- **Proactive Approach**: Early identification of issues requiring support
- **#OneTeam Response**: Collaborative problem-solving across all stakeholders
- **Customer Communication**: Transparent updates on challenges and resolutions

### Phase 4: Close and Transition

#### Deliverable Acceptance
- [ ] **Quality Validation**: Comprehensive review of all deliverables
- [ ] **Customer Approval**: Formal acceptance and sign-off process
- [ ] **Documentation**: Complete artifact repository and knowledge transfer
- [ ] **Compliance Verification**: Ensure all contractual obligations are met

#### Knowledge Transfer
- [ ] **Delivery to Sales (D2S)**: Transition insights back to sales team
- [ ] **Delivery to Operations (D2O)**: Hand-off to Enterprise Support if applicable
- [ ] **Lessons Learned**: Document best practices and improvement opportunities
- [ ] **Relationship Continuity**: Maintain customer relationship through transition

## Quality Standards and Metrics

### Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
- **Target**: Exceed customer expectations in all engagements
- **Measurement**: Post-engagement survey and feedback collection
- **Action**: Address any concerns proactively and implement improvements

### Delivery Performance
- **On-time Delivery**: Meet all committed milestones and deadlines
- **Budget Management**: Deliver within approved budget parameters
- **Quality Standards**: Exceed technical and business requirements
- **Risk Management**: Successfully mitigate identified risks

### Team Performance
- **Collaboration**: Effective #OneTeam working relationships
- **Communication**: Clear, timely, and professional stakeholder engagement
- **Innovation**: Creative problem-solving and value-added solutions
- **Learning**: Continuous skill development and knowledge sharing

## Implementation Checklist

### Pre-Engagement
- [ ] Complete Sales to Delivery (S2D) transition
- [ ] Conduct comprehensive Sprint 0 preparation
- [ ] Establish governance and communication structures
- [ ] Validate customer expectations and objectives

### During Engagement
- [ ] Maintain regular stakeholder communication
- [ ] Implement agile delivery practices
- [ ] Conduct ongoing quality inspections
- [ ] Proactively manage risks and issues

### Post-Engagement
- [ ] Obtain formal deliverable acceptance
- [ ] Complete knowledge transfer processes
- [ ] Conduct retrospective and lessons learned
- [ ] Transition relationship to appropriate teams
